Local Attractions

Ottawa features some amazing sites. Why not visit some while you're in town?

National Art Gallery

First constructed in 1880, this is one of Canada's premier art galleries.

Located at:
380 Sussex Drive

Parliament Buildings

Known by locals as The Hill, is an area of Crown land on the southern banks of the Ottawa River in downtown Ottawa that attracts approximately 3 million visitors each year.

Located :
111 Wellington Street

National Art Centre

The National Arts Centre is Ottawa primary centre for performing arts, located between Elgin Street and the Rideau Canal. The National Arts Centre was designated a National Historic Site of Canada in 2006.

Located :
53 Elgin St

Byward Market

Come to the market for brunch, lunch or dinner, and then hit the shops for the highest quality food products, fashions and cutting edge trends. See and be seen until the wee hours at one of our hip nightspots. Morning, noon and night the ByWard Market has something just for you.

Located :
55 ByWard Market Square

Become a speaker!

Are you ready for the challenge of speaking at Canada's most practical web professionals conference? Yes? Good! let's get started! Speaking at ACMELIVE! is a unique experience. Your talk must adhere to the following:

• Be 30 minutes in length
• Followed by 10 minutes of Q&A
• Overview of a web concept
• A deep dive into a part of that concept like a demo, code, or case study
• Give attendees practical takeaways
• Follow our Code of Conduct
• Free of commercial content, no in-depths of paid products or services, no promoting your employer

What you get

All speakers at ACMELIVE! will be paid a base honorarium of $500CAD, we will then reimburse you for travel and lodging expenses in Canadian Dollars, and free admission to the ACMELIVE! Conference.

You can be a tech speaker! We encourage everyone who has an idea for a talk to submit, we will help you ever step of the way to make it great, even making your submission better before we send it through our review process. We encourage under represented demographics to submit as well, we have and enforce a code of conduct to protect you and our attendees, and we have high standards for the type of content that is accepted in talks with our non-commercial policy and guidelines for creating a great talk.
